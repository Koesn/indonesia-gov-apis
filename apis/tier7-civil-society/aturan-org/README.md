# Aturan.org — Indonesian Regulation Finder

**Provider:** Independent third-party service  
**Base URL:** `https://aturan.org`  
**Primary Data Source:** `https://peraturan.bpk.go.id`

---

## Overview

Aturan.org is an Indonesian regulation database that provides **article-level semantic search** across Indonesian legislation.

Instead of searching PDF documents by keywords, Aturan.org retrieves the most relevant legal provisions (*pasal*) using semantic search, helping users locate the exact legal basis they need.

The platform supports two search modes:

- **Find Articles** — Search individual legal articles (*pasal*) using semantic search.
- **Find Regulations** — Search complete regulations and legal documents.

Regulation data is sourced from `https://peraturan.bpk.go.id`, processed into an article-level searchable database, and updated daily.

---

## Current Coverage

- **243,852 regulations**
- **4,543,462 legal articles (*pasal*)**

**Status:** Production-ready

---

## Key Features

- Semantic search designed specifically for Indonesian legal texts
- Article-level retrieval instead of document-level search
- Daily updates from the official regulation repository
- Fast access to relevant legal provisions without manually browsing PDFs
- Direct access to the original regulation PDF from every search result
